Let's make you fresher than ever

Clean N Classy is an exceptional cleaning outfit established and registered in Nigeria with a global standard pursuit. Our blend of services is carefully designed to enhance not only the physical ambiance and appearance of our clients but to further inspire confidence. Over the years, we have constantly demonstrated deep passion and desire for indisputable quality through a process of continuous re-invention and focus on fundamentals of good business ethics, strong customer relation and prompt delivery. Our services are bespoke and delivered in a warm and classy environment.

We are a team driven to be the clear leader delivering the utmost in customer service, building excellence performance while providing the best service at competitive prices. We aspire to be the leading company in the industry setting the pace and standards for others to follow. With our mission statement "to be the best at all times", we intend to create a market niche for uncompromised quality, excellence customer service and professionalism in all that we do at affordable rate thereby giving value for money.
