Carpet & Upholstery Cleaning

Carpets and upholsteries are expensive investment and constantly receiving high wear on a daily basis. Hence, there is need for a deliberate periodic maintenance in order to protect carpets and upholsteries from wearing-out in short time and further create the correct visual impression along with maintaining a healthy environment.
At Clean N Classy we use the most advanced equipment and eco-friendly chemicals for our carpet, rug and upholstery cleaning jobs. We help eliminate stains, dirt, bacteria and allergens, leaving your carpets looking their best whilst protecting their longevity and your investment.
